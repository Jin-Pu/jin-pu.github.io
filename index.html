about me

I am a PhD candidate in Signal Processing in Earth Observation, Technical University of Munich (TUM) & Remote Sensing Technology Institute (IMF), German Aerospace Center (DLR).

My research interests lie in the fields of temporal reasoning in high-dimensional data, such as remote sensing timeseries, and aerial videos. I have been involved in various projects, like aerial scene classification, aerial video classification, and anomaly detection in aerial videos. I am dedicated to observing our planet and making it better.
